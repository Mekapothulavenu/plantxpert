Frequently Asked Questions

PlantXpert Assistant Help

What is the PlantXpert Assistant?

The PlantXpert Assistant is an AI-powered chatbot that offers smart farming advice, crop care guidance, and real-time answers to your agricultural queries.

What can I ask the assistant?

You can ask about fertilizer recommendations, pesticide usage, crop-specific guidance, weather-based suggestions, and general plant care tips.

Is voice input or multi-language support available?

Currently, the assistant supports only text-based queries in English. We are working on expanding to voice and multilingual features in future updates.

Do I need to register to use the assistant?

Yes, user registration is required to access the assistant, so we can personalize your experience and store chat history for your reference.

Is the assistant available offline?

No, the assistant requires an internet connection to function, as it relies on cloud-based AI models for smart responses.

Can the assistant help with fertilizer and pesticide suggestions?

Yes, the assistant can recommend the most suitable fertilizers and pesticides based on your crop type and symptoms shared.

How does it provide weather-based suggestions?

The assistant uses real-time weather data to offer tips on irrigation, crop care, and disease prevention based on current conditions.

Can I delete chat messages or clear my chat history?

Yes, you can delete individual messages or clear your full chat history using the available options inside the assistant chat interface.

Is the assistant response time fast?

Yes, the assistant responds in real-time using a powerful AI model to ensure fast, accurate, and helpful replies.

How do I contact support for assistant-related issues?

If you face issues, reach out via email at support@plantxpert.com or use the chatbot for quick help.
