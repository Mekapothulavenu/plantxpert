Frequently Asked Questions

PlantXpert Help

What is PlantXpert?

PlantXpert is an AI-powered platform designed to detect plant diseases, provide weather updates, share agricultural news, and connect users with nearby agri-shops.

How does the plant disease detection feature work?

Users can upload an image of a plant, and our AI model analyzes it to detect possible diseases and suggest precautionary measures.

Is PlantXpert free to use?

Yes, the basic features such as plant disease detection, weather updates, chatbot support, and agri-news are free for all users.

How accurate is the AI in detecting plant diseases?

Our AI model is trained on a vast dataset and provides high accuracy, but we recommend consulting an agricultural expert for confirmation.

How can I find nearby agricultural shops?

Navigate to the "Agri Shops" section to view nearby stores, along with their locations on Google Maps.

Does PlantXpert provide weather updates?

Yes, we fetch real-time weather data from the OpenWeather API to help farmers make better agricultural decisions.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.

Can I get recommendations for plant care and disease prevention?

Yes, based on disease detection, PlantXpert provides precautionary measures and best practices for plant care.

How can I stay updated with the latest agricultural news?

Our news section fetches real-time agricultural updates from trusted sources via NewsAPI.

How do I contact support if I have an issue?

You can reach out to us via our chatbot or email support@plantxpert.com for assistance.
