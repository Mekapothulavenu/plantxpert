Crop Recommendation System Help

What is the Crop Recommendation System?

Itâ€™s an AI-based tool that suggests the most suitable crop to grow based on your soil nutrients and environmental conditions.

What details do I need to provide?

You need to enter Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium levels, Temperature, Humidity, pH value, and Rainfall to get accurate crop suggestions.

How is the recommended crop selected?

The system uses a trained machine learning model that analyzes your inputs and matches them with optimal growing conditions for various crops.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, the system processes your data online using cloud-based models to generate recommendations in real-time.

Is it suitable for all regions?

Yes, but the accuracy improves if you provide region-specific and up-to-date environmental data for your farmland.

Can I use it without logging in?

Currently, login is required to access the crop recommendation system, so we can save your data and suggestions for future reference.

How accurate are the recommendations?

The model has been trained on extensive agricultural data and provides high-accuracy suggestions, but field testing is recommended for best results.

Can it suggest multiple crops?

Currently, it suggests the single best crop. In future versions, we plan to offer a list of suitable crops based on your conditions.

Can I see the recommendation history?

Yes, after login, your previous crop suggestions will be saved to your profile for future viewing.

Who can I contact for technical support?

For any issues, email us at support@plantxpert.com or use the chatbot assistant for quick help.
